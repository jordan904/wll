Our Services

From lawn care to full landscape transformations, we bring your outdoor vision to life.

Retaining Walls

Custom-built stone and block retaining walls that add structure, prevent erosion, and create stunning multi-level landscapes.

Patios & Walkways

Interlocking paver patios, walkways, and driveways designed for beauty and durability. Built to withstand Maritime weather.

Landscape Design

Complete landscape design and installation — from garden beds and plantings to full backyard transformations that reflect your vision.

Concrete Work

Professional concrete pads, steps, and foundations. Precision-poured and finished for walkways, sheds, hot tubs, and more.

Excavation & Grading

Site preparation with mini excavators — grading, trenching, soil removal, and drainage solutions for any residential or commercial project.

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Weekly mowing, aeration, sod installation, overseeding, and seasonal cleanup. Keep your property looking its best year-round.

Built on Hard Work & Craftsmanship
